Openly accelerating science for all.

  • Free

    ADJECTIVE
    1. not subject to or constrained by engagements or obligation
    ADVERB
    1. not subject to or constrained by engagements or obligation

    We mean free. Scholarship should be available to the world and the world will determine its utility. The Open Journals platform allows scholars to share their work freely and allows others to review and read that work for free.
    No more gates.
    No more paywalls.

  • Accessible

    The Open Journals platform was created for everyone. Scholarship can be uploaded in any format and it can be accessed online or downloaded to read on other devices. The platform is also Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 compliant to ensure anyone can be a part of this scientific community.

  • Transparent

    Science is most beneficial when it is conducted in the open. The Open Journals platform allows scholars to share all their materials and encourages open communication between scholars and reviewers. The commitment to transparency applies to how we run the platform as well. All decisions, including financial and editorial, are publically disclosed.
